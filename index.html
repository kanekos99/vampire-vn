about
Vampire butler Cyrus is fed up with the antics of Mikael von Dietrich, the noble vampire lord that he serves, and he is determined to do whatever it takes to kill him.

Will his plan backfire spectacularly or will he finally be free from the clutches of his evil (and not handsome at all) vampire master?
features

  • Toxic vampire yaoi
  • Unserious plot and various shenanigans
  • 4 endings (1 good, 1 bad and 2 neutral)
  • 13k-14k words
  • 7 CGs (with variations)
