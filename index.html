about
Vampire butler Cyrus is fed up with the antics of Mikael von Dietrich, the noble vampire lord that he serves, and he is determined to do whatever it takes to kill him.

Will his plan backfire spectacularly or will he finally be free from the clutches of his evil (and not handsome at all) vampire master?
features
  • Gay vampires
  • Toxic vampire yaoi
  • Unserious plot and various shenanigans
  • 4 endings (1 good, 1 bad and 2 neutral)
  • 13k-14k words
  • 7 CGs (with variations)

dev notes
This Visual Novel was made for the SWAK 2026 and Queer Vampire Game Jams, based on the theme of "Toxic Love". It is made using Ren'Py, and features my original characters.

This is my first ever completed game/ VN and it is made in less than a month so please bear with me if it is a bit rough. (I all but gave up drawing CGs at the end.) But do let me know if you find any bugs and such!

This website itself is made to showcase this game on my personal website, and also to compile the stuff I've made in relation to this game. While you can play the game here, I would recommend checking it out on itch.io for a better experience.
